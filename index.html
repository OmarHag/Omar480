Keyboard Access Instructions

Use Tab to move between links and buttons. Press Enter to activate them.

This site focuses on making the navigation bar, list button, and reading links keyboard accessible with visible focus outlines.

Color Contrast & Accessibility

I used navy blue (#003366) on white for navigation and green (#00796B) for buttons to ensure high contrast.

All combinations tested with the WebAIM Contrast Checker passed WCAG AA and AAA guidelines.

I used Coblis to simulate color blindness and confirmed the site remains usable for Deuteranopia and Protanopia.
